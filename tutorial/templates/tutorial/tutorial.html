Rules of Tapa

  1. Blacken tiles to create a continuous path around the board.
  2. Use hints to determine the length of consecutive blackened tiles.
  3. If a hint uses more than one number then there must be a white tile between the blackened tiles.
  4. Blackened tiles must not form a 2x2 square anywhere on the board.
